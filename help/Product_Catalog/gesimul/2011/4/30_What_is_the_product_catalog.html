What is the product catalog?

2011. 4. 30.

 

Designing the product means detailing of product’s 6 components. Each components are generated by combination of design elements. For example, in ‘subscription’ component, in case customer subscribes this product and s/he will be charged at 20$ on ‘General Remains’ balance, subscribe/General Remains/20$ deduct can be derived from conditions/balance/actions elements each. Component can have more than one combination of design elements and designing component looks like making a tree structure with node element.

 

It explains what’s the meaning of product catalog.

UANGEL Product Catalog serves as a central design and management point operator’s product and services.

Figure below depicts main functions and components.

UA-Product Catalog server provides a operator UA-Product Catalog GUI tool(Client). Through this tool, person in charge designs a product(price plan). After creation, completed product are delivered to each network nodes such as customer care system, billing and rating system. Specially UA-Product Catalog interacts with UA-Rating System through compliable rule.

Product consists of 6 sub components as follows.


  1. ‣ Attribute

  2. Product has several default attributes - name, code, description, billing type(PPS, PostPaid), devices(ex, CellPhone, Notebook, Ebook), status(editing, requested, ...)

  3. ‣ Subscription

  4. It defines what to do during (un)subscription/suspending/transfering progress. For example, subscriber requirement - only person over 20 year-old, recurring amount subscription fee will be defined here.

  1. ‣ Tariff

  2. It defines what to do when customer use the specific services under certain conditions. For example, discount rate at night time, free SMS usage among friends

  3. ‣ Balance

  4. It defines which balance to be used under some conditions and priority among balances. For example, ‘Free Voice’ Balance has a higher priority to ‘General Remains’ balance.

  5. ‣ Promotion

  6. It defines which promotion to be enabled under certain conditions and discount/free rate in case it is enabled.

  7. ‣ VAS

  8. It defines which VASs to be added as default or optional and each VAS fee

Condition is the one of the major design elements to configure the product.


  1. ‣ Who

  2. -Member - Friend or Family, On/Off-net, Age and Gender

  3. -Number - Special phone number, ex) emergence calls - 119

  4. -Prefix - Special number starts with specific prefix, ex) international prefix - 070, 080, ...

  1. ‣ When

  2. -Duration - Special duration/term, ex)Christmas, New year and Thanks giving day

  3. -Week - weekdays, weekends, every Wednesday

  4. -Day - Special day, repeated date ex) 5th day per month

  5. -Timeslot - NIght time, peek time, ex) from 5:00 to 6:00

  6. ‣ Where

  7. -Country - country ex) korea, japan, mongolia

  8. -Zone - Specific regison ex) Ulaanbatar, Seoul, ...

  9. -Network - Used network, ex) GSM, CDMA, ...

  10. ‣ What

  11. -SMS, MMS, Voice and etc

  12. -Packet - QoS, URL Groups. ex) SNS sites (facebook, twitter)

Action is performed function to be activated under specific conditions.


  1. ‣ Deduct

  2. How amount of money/byte/count will be deducted. Ex) 20$ deduct, 30minutes deduct and 10 count deduct

  3. ‣ Discount

  4. Amount of percentage/byte/money/count will be discounted. Ex) 30% discount, 30minute discount

  5. ‣ Allow

  6. Free amount of byte/money/count. Ex) 30 count free, 5 minute free
