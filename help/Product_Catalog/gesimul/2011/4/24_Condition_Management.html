Condition Management

  1. ๏ Who

Condition is the one of the major design elements to configure the product. When certain actions (rate settings, etc.) occur,condition indicates the situation which each action performs on. Conditions consists of four domain - who, what, where and when. And each main condition domain is divied into sub-criteria.

  1. •Member : Relationship(Friend, Family), On-Net

  2. •Number : Specific target phone number (ex, 911)

  3. •Prefix: Specific phone number starts with (ex, 070, 080)

  1. ๏ What

  1. •Voice

  2. •SMS

  3. •Packet data service (sub-criterial: QoS, URL Groups)

  1. ๏ When

  1. •Duration - Specific date and terms (ex - new year, thanks giving day, christmas)

  2. •Weeks - Specific weeks (weekday, weekend)

  3. •Timeslots - Night time, peek time

  1. ๏ Where

  1. ๏ Overview

  1. •Country - Specific country

  2. •Zone - Specific region/province

  3. •Network - Network used (GSM, 3G CDMA)
