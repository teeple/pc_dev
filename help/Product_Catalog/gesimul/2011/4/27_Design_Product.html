First , just drag ‘New product’ element in left side bar (accordion menu) into design canvas  (displayed as ‘Drag Here’). And new window pops up.  This new window represents attribute of new product. Attributes contains product name/code, description, type (basic, optional, promotional, packaged), devices and status(editing, requested, ... )

Following movie clip depicts how to design subscription.

  1. ๏ Design Subscription

Subscription is defined as combination of conditions, balance and actions. Operator can drag several conditions in left-side menu and drop them into design canvas. Under selected conditions, operator choose and drag balance and actions in order.

  1. ๏ Design Tariff

Following movie clip depicts how to design tariffs.

Tariff is defined as combination of conditions and actions. Operator can drag several conditions in left-side menu and drop them into design canvas. Under selected conditions, operator choose actions and drag them under the specific condition.

Balance is defined as selecting balances, used conditions and prioritizing them. Operator can drag balance in left-side menu and drop it into design canvas. Priority in higher position in tree is higher than it in lower position. Operator drag and drop conditions into each balance. And it means that balance is used under specific conditions.

Following movie clip depicts how to design promotion.

  1. ๏ Design Balance

Promotion is defined as completing required factors in each promotion. Operator can drag template promotion in left-side menu and drop it into design canvas. Under selected template promotion, operator complete each factor.

Following movie clip depicts how to design promotion.

  1. ๏ Design Promotion

Operator can drag one of value-added services in left-side menu and drop it into design canvas. VAS design canvas are divided into two category - default and optional. VASs in default category are subscribed as given product’s default VASs and those in optional category can be selected by customer optionally.

Following movie clip depicts how to design value-added services.

  1. ๏ Design VAS

After creation, automatically overview tab in right side bar menu will be activated and product tree view will be expanded and show. Operator can zoom in/out the product tree and can see the product tree as several view - vertical tree view(default), horizontal tree view and table view.
