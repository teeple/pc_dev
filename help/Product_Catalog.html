How to Design Product

 
 

UANGEL Product Catalog serves as a central design and management point operator’s product and services.

Figure below depicts main functions and components.

 

  1. ๏ Overview

UA-Product Catalog server provides a operator UA-Product Catalog GUI tool(Client). Through this tool, person in charge designs a product(price plan). After creation, completed product are delivered to each network nodes such as customer care system, billing and rating system. Specially UA-Product Catalog interacts with UA-Rating System through rule.

Condition is the one of the major design elements to configure the product.

  1. ‣ Who

  2. -Member - Friend or Family, On/Off-net, Age and Gender

  3. -Number - Special phone number, ex) emergence calls - 119

  4. -Prefix - Special number starts with specific prefix, ex) international prefix - 070, 080, ...

  5. ‣ When

  6. -Duration - Special duration/term, ex)Christmas, New year and Thanks giving day

  7. -Week - weekdays, weekends, every Wednesday

  8. -Day - Special day, repeated date ex) 5th day per month

  9. -Timeslot - NIght time, peek time, ex) from 5:00 to 6:00

  10. ‣ Where

  11. -Country - country ex) korea, japan, mongolia

  12. -Zone - Specific regison ex) Ulaanbatar, Seoul, ...

  13. -Network - Used network, ex) GSM, CDMA, ...

  14. ‣ What

  15. -SMS, MMS, Voice and etc

  16. -Packet - QoS, URL Groups. ex) SNS sites (facebook, twitter)
